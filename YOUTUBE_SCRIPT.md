## THE TELOMERE CAP (2:00 — 2:30)

> **Narrator (V/O):**
> "But this cap isn't enforced by an arbitrary line of code. It's enforced by biology."

**[Visual: Telomere counter ticking down: 21,000,000 → 20,999,950 → ...]**

> "The network's DNA has telomeres — TTAGGG repeats, just like human chromosomes. Every coin mined shortens them. When they're gone, mining stops forever. The Hayflick limit. 21 million coins. Then cellular senescence."

**[Visual: The chromosome telomeres reach zero. Text appears: "No more coins can ever be created."]**

## THE PATERNITY TEST (2:30 — 3:10)

**[Visual: Side by side — "Network" lane, "Coin #1: MATCH" (green check), "Forged coin: NO MATCH" (red X).]**

> "It's a DNA paternity test for money. And it works completely offline."

**[Visual: Shield 3 — Gel bands, purple glow.]**
